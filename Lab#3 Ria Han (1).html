I want to show the case that in the next five years, milennials will not have as many children with the career becoming more prevalent among milennial women, as they value more independence and privacy. The multiple regression shows that for older woman, statistically significant variables was spdeg( spouse's highest educational degree). there is higher 0.045 points increase in spouse's degree as fewer children respondents had. This came out to be the opposite to what I had as a hypothesis, which is that the higher financial situation and education the higher the fertility, but there could be an error with random sampling. The age, marital status, and the level of improvement in one's financial sitaution have positive relationship with the fertility. Given as the financial situation improved more, lower fertility on average. This once again seems ironic to what my hypothesis was, which is that the higher income one has the more fertility one will have.¶
